¿Cuáles son los 3 departamentos que más gastan en salarios actualmente?

`SELECT department_id, sum(salary) as suma_salarios FROM employees GROUP BY department_id ORDER BY suma_salarios DESC fetch first 3 rows only;`

Devolver las siguientes columnas:  employee_id, first_name, last_name,  salary, job_id ordenados  por el apellido del empleado.

`SELECT employee_id, first_name, last_name, salary, job_id FROM employees ORDER BY last_name;`

Devolver las siguientes columnas:  employee_id, first_name, last_name,  salary de la tabla employees y job_title de la tabla jobs.

`SELECT employees.employee_id, employees.first_name, employees.last_name, employees.salary, jobs.job_title FROM employees JOIN jobs ON employees.job_id=jobs.job_id;`

Reescribir la instrucción anterior usando alias:

`SELECT e.employee_id, e.first_name, e.last_name, e.salary, j.job_title FROM employees e JOIN jobs j ON e.job_id=j.job_id ORDER BY e.last_name`

Devolver además de los campos anteriores el campo department_name de la tabla departments:

`SELECT  e.employee_id,e.first_name,e.last_name,e.salary,j.job_title,d.department_name  FROM employees e JOIN jobs j ON j.job_id=e.job_id JOIN departments d ON d.department_id=e.department_id`

Contar cuántos empleados tiene cada departamento, agrupado por país, ordenado de mayor a menor cantidad dentro de cada país.

`SELECT co.country_name, d.department_name, COUNT(e.employee_id) AS cantidad_empleados FROM employees e JOIN departments d  ON e.department_id = d.department_id JOIN locations   l  ON d.location_id   = l.location_id JOIN countries   co ON l.country_id    = co.country_id GROUP BY co.country_name, d.department_name ORDER BY co.country_name, cantidad_empleados DESC;`
